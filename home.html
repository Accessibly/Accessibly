How to use Accessibly

Accessible

A green pin on our map represents an accessible location. These are places with full access. Locations might not have automatic doors, but we can get in, party, and go home with no problem.

Partially Accessible

An amber pin on our map represents a location that is partially accessible. Amber locations often have alternative entrances or limited access within the space, such as steps to the bar area.

Not Accessible

A red pin on our map represents a location that is not accessible. Find a red pin on our map? Why not tweet @AccessiblyAU about it and let’s make change happen.
